Strategically nestled in the heart of Badlapur, the finest residential project offered by Shashwat Group give you honour living at its base. A harmonious assemble of design, architecture and luxury, the meticulously design comfort apartments in Badlapur take your lifestyle to altogether different level. By giving the double privilege of comfort and style, this masterpieces are thoughtful crafted for those who wish the final things in life.

Shashwat Park is spread over 10 acres of land , offer 32 towers each of seven stored in combination of 1BHK and 2BHK. The project consists of 1,500 apartments, clubhouse, landscape, garden, well equipped gymnasium, jogging track and meditation center etc
